Welcome to LIFT

The Literacy & Innovation in Financial Technology Alliance (LIFT) is a nonprofit organization committed to demystifying the world of digital finance. We provide resources and education to help individuals and communities understand and leverage technologies like distributed ledger technology, digital assets, and decentralized systems for a more equitable financial future.

Our Focus Areas

Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT)

Understanding the fundamentals of DLT, its various consensus mechanisms, and its transformative potential across various industries.

Digital Assets & Cryptocurrencies

Exploring the evolving world of digital assets, from cryptocurrencies to non-fungible tokens (NFTs), and their impact on finance.

Decentralized Systems

Learning about decentralized networks, Web3, and their role in creating more open and transparent digital ecosystems.
