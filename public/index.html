Welcome to LIFT

The Literacy & Innovation in Financial Technology Alliance (LIFT) is a nonprofit organization committed to demystifying the world of digital finance. We provide resources and education to help individuals and communities understand and leverage technologies like distributed ledger technology, digital assets, and decentralized systems for a more equitable financial future.
