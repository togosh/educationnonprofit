Governance and Transparency

Our Commitment to Transparency

LIFT is committed to operating with the highest standards of transparency and accountability. We believe that making our governance documents and financial information publicly available is essential for building trust with our supporters, partners, and the community we serve. This page provides access to key documents and information about our organization's structure and operations.

Governing Documents

Our governance is guided by the following documents, which are available for public review:

  • LIFT Organizational Bylaws
  • Conflict of Interest Policy

Board of Directors

LIFT is led by a dedicated volunteer board committed to our educational mission:

  • Stephen - President & Treasurer
  • Victor - Vice President
  • Sarah - Secretary

Board members serve in a volunteer capacity and are subject to our Conflict of Interest policy. Information is provided for transparency.

Public Filings

As part of our commitment to transparency, our IRS Form 1023 application for tax-exempt status and our future annual IRS Form 990 filings will be made available for public inspection on this page upon receipt and filing.
