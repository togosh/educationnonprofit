LIFT Holds First Official Board Meeting

Posted on June 28, 2025

We are thrilled to announce that the Literacy & Innovation in Financial Technology Alliance (LIFT) held its first official board meeting, marking a significant milestone in our journey. The meeting established the foundational governance and operational framework for our organization.

Key Outcomes from the Meeting:

  • Adoption of Governing Policies: The board officially adopted our corporate Bylaws and a Conflict of Interest Policy, ensuring a strong ethical and operational foundation.

  • Election of Directors: We are proud to introduce our founding Board of Directors: Stephen, Victor, and Sarah. Their combined expertise will be invaluable in guiding LIFT's mission.

  • Appointment of Officers: The board appointed our executive officers to lead day-to-day operations:
    • President, Treasurer: Stephen Cernota
    • Vice President: Victor Medcalf
    • Secretary: Sarah Salazar

  • Operational Authorizations: The board authorized the opening of a bank account to manage our finances and formally approved the filing of Form 1023 to seek 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status from the IRS.

These foundational steps are critical as we work to build a transparent, accountable, and effective organization dedicated to financial technology education. We are energized by the progress and excited for the path ahead.

Thank you for your continued support. Stay tuned for more updates as we continue to build and grow!
