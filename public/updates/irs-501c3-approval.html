LIFT Receives IRS 501(c)(3) Approval

Posted on October 4, 2025

We are thrilled to announce that the Literacy & Innovation in Financial Technology Alliance (LIFT) has officially been recognized by the Internal Revenue Service as a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt public charity! This is a momentous achievement for our organization and marks a significant milestone in our mission to provide accessible financial technology education.

What This Means for LIFT

Receiving 501(c)(3) status confirms that LIFT operates exclusively for educational and charitable purposes. This recognition brings several important benefits:

  • Tax-Deductible Donations: Contributions to LIFT are now tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. Donors can support our mission while benefiting from potential tax advantages.
  • Enhanced Credibility: IRS recognition as a 501(c)(3) organization validates our commitment to transparency, accountability, and public service.
  • Expanded Opportunities: This status opens doors to grant opportunities and partnerships that will help us expand our educational programs and reach more communities.
  • Public Accountability: As a recognized public charity, we are held to rigorous standards of transparency and governance, which aligns perfectly with our commitment to operating in the public interest.

Our Commitment to Transparency

In keeping with our dedication to transparency, we have made our IRS determination letter publicly available on our governance page. This document confirms our tax-exempt status and provides important information about our organization's structure and purpose.

Thank You to Our Supporters

We are deeply grateful to everyone who has supported LIFT on this journey. From our dedicated board members to our early donors and community supporters, this achievement would not have been possible without you. Your belief in our mission has helped us reach this important milestone.

Looking Ahead

With our 501(c)(3) status now confirmed, we are more committed than ever to expanding our educational resources and reaching more people across the United States. We will continue to develop high-quality, unbiased educational content about financial technologies, distributed ledger systems, and digital assets.

Our work continues with the same dedication to excellence, transparency, and public service that has guided us from the beginning. We look forward to the future and the positive impact we can make together.

If you would like to support our mission with a tax-deductible donation, please visit our donation page.

Thank you for being part of the LIFT community!
