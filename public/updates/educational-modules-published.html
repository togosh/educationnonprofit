New Educational Modules Published

Posted on June 18, 2025

We are excited to announce the publication of 11 comprehensive educational modules as part of LIFT's core curriculum! These resources are designed to guide learners from foundational concepts in blockchain and digital assets to more advanced topics like DeFi, DAOs, and digital asset taxation.

What’s Included?

  • Module 1: Introduction to Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) & Blockchain
  • Module 2: Navigating Digital Assets
  • Module 3: Understanding Decentralized Systems & Web3
  • Module 4: Digital Asset Security & Self-Custody Basics
  • Module 5: Smart Contracts Explained
  • Module 6: Decentralized Finance (DeFi) – The Building Blocks
  • Module 7: Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) – Technology & Beyond
  • Module 8: Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs)
  • Module 9: How to Use a Block Explorer
  • Module 10: An Educational Overview of Digital Asset Taxation
  • Module 11: Comparing Blockchain Ecosystems (A Case Study Approach)

Why This Matters

Our mission is to make financial technology education accessible, objective, and easy to understand. These modules are free to access and are designed for learners of all backgrounds—no prior experience required.

We encourage you to explore the full curriculum and share it with anyone who may benefit from a better understanding of digital finance and emerging technologies.
