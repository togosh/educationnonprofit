LIFT Officially Creates Nonprofit Corporation

Posted on June 13, 2025

We are excited to announce that the Literacy & Innovation in Financial Technology Alliance (LIFT) is now officially recognized as a nonprofit corporation in the State of Texas! Our formation documents were filed and accepted by the Texas Secretary of State, and our entity now appears in the Texas Comptroller's public records.

How We Did It

We filed our Certificate of Formation as a nonprofit corporation through the Texas Secretary of State, using Northwest Registered Agent as our registered agent. The filing fee was $65, and we received confirmation of our filing and entity status shortly after submission.

Why This Matters

  • Legal Recognition: LIFT is now a legally recognized nonprofit corporation, which is a key step toward our mission of providing accessible, high-quality financial technology education.
  • Transparency: Our status can be verified by anyone through the Texas Comptroller's website, ensuring public trust and accountability.
  • Next Steps: With our Texas nonprofit status in place, we are now eligible to apply for federal 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status and continue building our educational programs.

Thank you to everyone who has supported us so far. We are excited to continue our journey as an official nonprofit and look forward to sharing more updates soon!
