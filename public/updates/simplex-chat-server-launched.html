LIFT Launches SimpleX Chat Server for Secure Communication

Posted on October 4, 2025

We are excited to announce that LIFT has launched its own SimpleX Chat server, providing a secure, private, and decentralized communication channel for our community members, supporters, and anyone interested in learning about financial technology.

What is SimpleX Chat?

SimpleX Chat is a privacy-focused messaging platform that prioritizes security and anonymity. Unlike traditional messaging apps, SimpleX:

  • Requires no phone numbers or email addresses – protecting your identity from the start
  • Has no user identifiers – not even a user ID that could be traced
  • Uses end-to-end encryption – ensuring your messages remain private
  • Is decentralized – giving users control over their communication infrastructure

Why We're Using SimpleX

As an organization dedicated to financial technology education, we believe in practicing what we teach. Digital privacy and security are foundational concepts in the Web3 and decentralized technology space. By hosting our own SimpleX server, we're:

  • Demonstrating the practical application of decentralized communication technology
  • Providing a secure space for privacy-conscious individuals to engage with our content and community
  • Supporting open-source, privacy-preserving technologies that align with our educational mission
  • Offering an alternative to traditional, data-harvesting communication platforms

How to Join Our SimpleX Chat

Connecting with LIFT via SimpleX Chat is simple:

  1. Download the SimpleX Chat app – Available for iOS, Android, and desktop platforms at simplex.chat
  2. Scan our QR code or click the connection link on our Contact page
  3. Start the conversation – Ask questions, share ideas, or learn more about digital finance in a secure environment
