Why Your Support Matters

Literacy & Innovation in Financial Technology Alliance (LIFT) is committed to providing free and accessible educational resources to help people understand emerging digital technologies. Your generous contributions directly fund:

  • Development of clear, unbiased educational guides and materials (including direct mail).
  • Creation of online learning modules.
  • Hosting of community workshops and informational sessions.
  • Website maintenance and outreach efforts.

Together, we can empower our communities with the knowledge needed for the digital future.

How to Donate

LIFT is applying for 501(c)(3) nonprofit status. Once approved, donations will be tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. We are currently setting up our donation processing systems.

All contributions will be used to support LIFT's tax-exempt educational programs and operations.

No goods or services will be provided in exchange for donations.
