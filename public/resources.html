Our Learning Modules (Examples - To Be Developed)

DFEAT is developing the following educational content. Check back for updates!

Module 1: Introduction to Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT)

What is DLT? How does it differ from traditional databases? Understanding core concepts like blocks, chains, and consensus mechanisms. Exploring different types of DLTs. (Content Coming Soon)

Module 2: Understanding Decentralized Systems

Exploring peer-to-peer networks, decentralized applications (dApps), and the philosophy behind decentralization. Case studies of different approaches to building decentralized ecosystems. (Content Coming Soon)

Module 3: Digital Assets Explained

A neutral overview of what digital assets are, different categories (e.g., native network tokens, utility tokens, NFTs from a technology perspective), and how they function within their respective networks. This module will include objective explanations of how systems like are designed to operate, without investment commentary. (Content Coming Soon)

Module 4: Navigating Digital Ecosystems Safely

Understanding common security practices, recognizing potential risks, and resources for staying safe when interacting with digital platforms. (Content Coming Soon)
