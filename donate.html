Support Our Educational Mission

Why Your Support Matters

Digital Future Education Alliance of Texas (DFEAT) is committed to providing free and accessible educational resources to help people understand emerging digital technologies. Your generous contributions directly fund:

  • Development of clear, unbiased educational guides and materials (including direct mail).
  • Creation of online learning modules.
  • Hosting of community workshops and informational sessions.
  • Website maintenance and outreach efforts.

Together, we can empower our communities with the knowledge needed for the digital future.

How to Donate
